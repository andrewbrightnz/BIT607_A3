Do you want your dog to look snazzy?

Snazzy Paws Grooming is here to help....

Snazzy Paws Grooming are a professional dog grooming service based near Hamilton Lake. Snazzy Paws Grooming was started by Fred and Wilma Bedrock, a couple who are passionate about dogs and aim to provide top-notch grooming services to locals and pet owners in the Hamilton region.

We have two fulltime staff (Barney and Betty) and our grooming services include a variety of packages such as bathing, clipping, nail trimming and specialised treatments.

We're sure you'll find our service professional, great value, and reliable.

Snazzy Paws Grooming privacy policy

We collect personal information from you, including your:

  • name
  • contact information

We collect your personal information in order to:

  • record relevant information about our clients and their pets
  • know how to contact our clients

Providing information is optional. If you choose not to enter contact information, we'll make do without it - no stress!

We keep your information safe by storing it in password protected accounts and vetting any staff that will access it.

We keep your information for six months after we last heard from you, at which point we securely destroy it by securely erasing it.

You have the right to ask for a copy of any personal information we hold about you, and to ask for it to be corrected if you think it is wrong.

If you’d like to ask for a copy of your information, or to have it corrected, please contact us at:

  • Email us using this form
  • Phone: 020-123-4567
  • Post: 89 Lake Domain Drive, Frankton, Hamilton, NEW ZEALAND.

We're open: 9–5 Monday to Saturday, but we're closed on public holidays
